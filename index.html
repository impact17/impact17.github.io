Partnering for a Better World

Impact17 promotes and supports multi-sector and multi-stakeholder partnerships to deliver the Sustainable Development Goals and improve humanitarian action.

Learn More

Our Vision

A better world, in which we see more collaboration and less isolation.

The fastest path to achieving the world we want by 2030 is by moving from short-term, small-scale partnership projects, to long-term, transformational, multi-stakeholder partnerships with the potential for scalable impact.

Our Goal

We are a social enterprise partnering with UN Agencies, academia, partnership experts and practitioners to build a greater understanding of partnerships for the SDGs. We convene partnering organisations around common challenges to overcome them and create opportunities.

We are here to help companies, governments and civil society partner better.
