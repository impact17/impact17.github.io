Partnering with WHO to respond to the COVID-19 crisis

Impact17 and Alliance4Impact have been participating in WHO coordinated outreach in response to COVID-19 since the beginning of the crisis.

To facilitate the response, WHO has requested that we share the document “Asks to the private sector in the response to COVID-19”

The document contains guidance on how to protect business against COVID-19, and how to help support the response. It also include references to WHO and other resources that are useful to businesses.


Combatting COVID-19

Impact17, in partnership with Alliance4Impact and Meaningful Business, also hosted a digital discussion series on business response to COVID-19. Each discussion focused on solutions to the pressing challenges during COVID-19.

The intention was for business leaders to share what they are currently doing, and to understand what individual and collective action might be useful.
