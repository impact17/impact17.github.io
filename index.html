Our Services

We offer a large range of services, such as

Evaluate

We use advanced digital technologies and primary research to conducted tailored mapping of who is doing what to contribute to improve humanitarian response and deliver the Sustainable Development Goals. We evaluate multi-sector partnerships, assess performance and publish learning.

Promote

We promote partnerships and encourage action, by increasing public awareness about challenges and opportunities. We increase transparency by encouraging organisations to share experience and learning about their partnerships.

Support

We provide organisations a curated menu of services from credible Impact17 Associate Partners tailored to their specific needs, including: advice, evaluation, and the brokering of partnerships, digital tools to support partnering, risk assessments and complex system impact assessments.
