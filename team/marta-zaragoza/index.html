Marta Zaragoza Navarro

  • January 1, 0001

Researcher

After having been based in Berkeley and Geneva, Marta is currently enrolled in a Dual Master’s Degree in International Public Management and International Relations jointly offered by Sciences Po Paris and Peking University. With a strong interest in SDG 17 Partnerships for the Goals, Marta is passionate about bringing together unlikely allies and building bridges across perceived boundaries. She is and has been involved in numerous youth parliaments, organizations and delegations at a national and international level. In 2015 she was appointed as a young ambassador for children’s rights by the NGO Terre des Hommes, in 2017 as the Swiss UNESCO youth representative and, in 2018, she was invited to join the Global Changemakers network. She is now establishing Occasio, an association aiming to foster active citizenship by helping young people in Switzerland catalyze positive social change. Marta is contributing to the Impact17 fundraising strategy and development of online training.
