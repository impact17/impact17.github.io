About Impact17

Connect, collaborate and communicate to create and support high-quality partnerships.

Our story

Among the 17 Sustainable Development Goals, there is one goal that is essential for achieving all of the others. SDG17 calls on the global community to:

Strengthen the means of implementation and revitalize the global partnership for sustainable development.

The name Impact17 represents our mutually beneficial objective of helping organisations from different sectors of society involved in partnerships be better able to achieve immediate sustainable impact before 2030 in adherence with the values and principles of the SDGs.

Together with our Associate Partners, we have built a menu of tailored services in collaboration with high quality experienced experts and practitioners to promote and support partnerships for the SDGs.

Our Vision

A better world, in which we see more collaboration and less isolation.

Our Goal

More effective partnerships that achieve sustainable impact.

Our Objective

To evaluate, promote and support partnerships for social good.

We are here to help companies, governments and civil society partner better.

We are building a community of like-minded organisations that believe in the power of collaboration.
