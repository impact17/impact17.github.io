Researcher

Roshni is volunteering as a Researcher for Impact 17. Having witnessed the inequalities that exist whilst growing up in India and East Africa, she is passionate about facilitating sustainable development that create level playing fields for individuals to tap into their potential. She is currently based in Geneva on a Fellowship with the World Economic Forum to support the Closing the Gender Gap Accelerators; public private collaborations that address the closing of the economic gender gap. Roshni brings over 15+ years consulting experience to multinationals in helping to design and execute multi-country programs. Through her volunteering at Impact17, she is keen to build a better understanding of how cross-sector partnerships can be effectively ran to bring about sustainable systemic change. Roshni is a Fellow of the Institute and Faculty of Actuaries specialised in Finance & Investment and holds an MBA from INSEAD. In her free time, she enjoys travelling and pursuing her interest in Bharatanatyam, an Indian classical dance form.
